About Us

ABC Public School is a co-educational institution dedicated to providing quality education in a safe, disciplined, and nurturing environment. The school focuses on academic excellence, moral values, physical development, and creative learning. With experienced teachers , modern infrastucture , and a balanced blend of academics and co-curricular activities , we strive to bring out the best in every learner. Academics

Curriculum & Board Affiliation

ABC Public School follows the CBSE curriculum and strictly adheres to the guidelines issued by the Central Board of Secondary Education. The school is an English-medium institution and emphasizes conceptual clarity, analytical thinking, and practical application of knowledge.

Teaching Methodology

Our teaching approach is student-centric and aligned with the New Education Policy (NEP). Teachers use smart classrooms, digital tools, activity-based learning, and regular assessments to ensure effective learning outcomes.

  • Smart classrooms and digital learning resources
  • Regular tests, assignments, and feedback
  • Project-based and experiential learning
  • Special doubt-clearing and remedial classes
  • Preparation for competitive and board examinations

Evaluation System

The school follows Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) to assess students on scholastic and co-scholastic parameters, ensuring balanced academic and personal growth.
